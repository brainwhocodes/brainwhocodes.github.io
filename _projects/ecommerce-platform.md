## Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive e-commerce platform for a retail brand looking to expand their online presence.

## Challenge

The client needed a scalable solution that could handle high traffic volumes during peak shopping seasons while maintaining a seamless user experience.

## Solution

We developed a custom e-commerce platform using React for the frontend and Node.js for the backend, with the following features:

- Real-time inventory management
- Secure payment processing
- Mobile-responsive design
- Advanced search and filtering
- Customer account management

## Results

The platform successfully handled 10x traffic during the holiday season with zero downtime, resulting in a 150% increase in online sales.
