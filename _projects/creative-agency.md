## Overview

A bold, creative portfolio website designed to showcase the agency's work and attract new clients.

## Design Philosophy

The design emphasizes the agency's creative approach with:

- Full-screen video backgrounds
- Smooth scroll animations
- Case study presentations
- Team member profiles
- Contact form integration

## Impact

The new website helped the agency secure 5 new major clients within the first month of launch.
